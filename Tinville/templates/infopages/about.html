Payment Information

Getting paid on TInville is made simple thanks to our partnership with Stripe. Using a checking account or debt card will allow you to create a shop on Tinville and get paid for all products sold through your shop Designers will only receive payment for their order (minus Tinville costs) upon confirmation of shipping out the product(s) to the buyer. It is also up to the designer to ensure accuracy of payment information submitted to Tinville and Stripe. *Please note: To sign up as designer on Tinville you must use a valid checking account or debit card credentials. All other forms of payment will not be accepted and may cause your shop to be modified or deleted.
